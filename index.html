What we do?

Sustainable Fashion

Did you know that nearly 90% from all the donated clothes end up in the landfill. We are changing this by giving pre-loved jeans second life. All our denim pairs are washed and then repaired if required.

Denim Expertice

With the help of our denim experts, you can find your favourite new pair of jeans. We will guide you in finding the right fabric and fit. You can select from various styles and brands that we have in store. We even offer our own repair and alteration service, so rest assure that you will find that perfect piece that will fit you. For more information about repair and alteration see services tab.

Unique Items

We have a vast collection of jeans from known brands to less know designer pieces. If you are after a unique vintage pair or limited-edition design or just a pair of extra soft worn in classics we have some options for you.
