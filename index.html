ReDenim

A boutique re-selling and repurposing second hand jeans, located in the heart of Edinburgh.

Sustainable Fashion

About 90% from all the unwanted clothes end up in landfill. We aim to reduce this number and to give pre-loved jeans a second chance.

Denim Expertice

Our denim experts can help you find the new favourite pair of jeans. We even offer our own repair and alteration service, so we can quarantee the best fit.

Unique Items

We have a vast collection of jeans from known brands to less know designer pieces. If you are after a unique vintage pair or limited-edition design or classic styles, we have some options for you.
